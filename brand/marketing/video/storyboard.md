# claude-resume — Marketing Video Storyboard

**Runtime**: ~60–90 seconds
**Style**: Dark terminal aesthetic, cinematic text reveals, ambient electronic music
**Tone**: Technical credibility. Not hype. The kind of video a senior engineer would share.

---

## Music Direction

**Track feel**: Ambient/electronic with tension build. Think Bonobo, Jon Hopkins, or Tycho.
- Opens: sparse, slow-building — a few notes, space between them
- Middle: rhythm kicks in as speed/numbers are revealed
- Climax: full groove when the search runs under 1 second
- Outro: resolves soft, confident

**AI Music tools**: Suno (`/create`) or Udio — prompt: *"ambient electronic instrumental, dark minimal, slow build, cinematic, 90 BPM, Bonobo-style"*

## Production Notes

### AI Video Tools
- **Runway Gen-3** or **Sora**: For the "person typing" and terminal footage transitions
- **HeyGen** or **Synthesia**: Optional — Daniel's face/voice narration layer over the text reveals
- **CapCut** or **DaVinci Resolve**: Final assembly, text animation, color grading

### Text Animation Style
- Font: JetBrains Mono or IBM Plex Mono (matches terminal aesthetic)
- Color: White text on #0a0a0a background, with `#00ff88` (green) for key numbers
- Transitions: Typewriter for code blocks, fade-in for prose, counter animation for numbers

### Terminal Footage
- Use actual claude-resume terminal sessions (record with `asciinema`, render with `svg-term`)
- Real data is more credible than mockups — the 0.604s timing is a real benchmark

### Duration Variants
- **90s full version**: LinkedIn article video / YouTube
- **30s cut**: LinkedIn feed / Twitter
- **15s cut**: Instagram Reels / TikTok hook
